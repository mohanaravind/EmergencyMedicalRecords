User Agreement

This application can be used to store your critical medical information in your device which can be useful during emergency. Maintain your personal online emergency medical record securely through 2 factor authentication. Add emergency contacts and let them receive your latitude, longitude, critical medical records as well as access to your personal emergency medical record through a simple SMS. Sending an SOS message to your contacts is as simple as a press on your device. Android widgets makes it easier to use during emergency.
